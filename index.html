About the school

Jupe Hills is a boys and girls school at Ibaya, Ilalila — in Shibula ward, Buswelu, Ilemela District, Mwanza. We teach children from day care through Standard IV.

The school is committed to developing pupils and children of all kinds, all religions and all races. We believe all people are God's creation, and each child is supported in belief according to their own religion.

Children aged 2–5 join our day care. Children aged 6–7 join Standard I, and a child is interviewed before admission into Classes I, II, III and IV. Learn more about our school or read how to join us.

Programmes

Day care

Ages 2 – 5

Our youngest children are cared for through the school day — day care opens from 7:00. Children play, learn and rest in a safe, supervised place while parents work.

Pre-primary

Baby Class · Middle Class · Pre-Unit

Children learn letters, numbers, songs and how to be part of a class — everything they need to be ready for Standard I.

Primary

Standard I – IV · join Standard I at ages 6 – 7

Full primary teaching from Standard I to Standard IV. A child is interviewed before admission into Classes I, II, III and IV.
