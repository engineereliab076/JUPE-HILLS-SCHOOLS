How to apply

Admission process

  1. Contact the school

    Call, WhatsApp or email the school with the child’s age and current class.

  2. Visit with the child

    Meet the school team, see the campus and complete any required interview or readiness check.

  3. Submit documents and confirm a place

    Bring the requested records, receive fee guidance and complete registration.

Prepare for your visit

Documents to bring

  • Copy of the child’s birth certificate
  • Two recent passport-size photographs
  • Previous school report or transfer information, where applicable
  • Parent or guardian contact details
  • Any relevant health or support information

Current joining guidance

Contact the school for the latest joining instructions, placement guidance and approved requirements.

Common questions

Admissions FAQ

Does Jupe Hills admit children from every religion?

Yes. The school welcomes all religions and supports each child with respect for their family’s beliefs.

Is an interview always required?

An interview or readiness assessment may be required before class placement.

Can we visit before applying?

Yes. Call ahead and the school can arrange a suitable time for your family to visit.

Are fees listed online?

Not yet. Contact the school for the current approved fee schedule and payment information.
